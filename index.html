❓ How to Play

👨‍🌾You are a natural resource manager responsible for protecting this habitat. Use science-based interventions to manage the invasive species invasion.
🃏Your hand holds two kinds of cards: controls (🔨✂️🐾💧🔥💊) that remove invaders, and green native enhancements (🌱🌻🌾🤝) that plant and protect natives. Tap a card, then tap its target - or drag it.
🎯Every invader has weaknesses based on real conservation science. Use the wrong method and it survives! Check the 📖 Field Guide.
🌙When your actions run out, end the turn - but beware: invaders spread to neighboring cells.
🦋Native species (gold rings) die if surrounded by invaders. Plant more and restore habitat to keep your ratio healthy!
🏆Win by maintaining a healthy ecosystem (more natives than invaders) for 6 consecutive turns. Lose if the invasion meter fills up or all natives are lost.
💡 Once a species becomes invasive, complete eradication is nearly impossible. Management is about maintaining a balance. Tap any creature to learn about it.
